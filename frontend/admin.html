Police Patrol Planner

Select 3–6 patrol checkpoints to plan the most efficient patrol circuit:

Planning patrol routes for many checkpoints is extremely complex. The quick method gives a good route fast, while the optimal method guarantees the best route but only works for small sets (≤6 checkpoints).
Algorithm: Travelling Salesman Problem (TSP)
Quick = Nearest Neighbour Greedy O(n²), Optimal = Brute Force O(n!)
TSP is NP-Hard — no known polynomial-time exact solution
